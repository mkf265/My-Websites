What:  We are excited to kick off the New Year by partnering up again with NPN! Concentric therapists will be writing monthly blogs on various topics involving parenting, self-care, couples and marriage challenges, and mental health. Check them out at the top of the month at Neighborhood Parents Network Blog.

When:  January 2017 - Ongoing

What:  As an alumna of Argosy University's Arizona School of Professional Psychology -Phoenix, Concentric President Jennifer Larson, LCPC, NCC is profiled in this "Success Story" on Argosy website.

When:  April 2016

What:  Check out Neighborhood Parents Network (NPN) Blog -- 10 THINGS TO DO WHEN YOUR PARTNER'S HAD AN AFFAIR written by Jennifer Larson.  

When:  March 2016


What:  We are excited to announce that providing counseling services to students at Sauganash School has gone well, that we are expanding donated services to include direct psycho-education and support services to teachers who may benefit from learning how social, emotional, and behavioral needs of children impact academics and well-being in their classrooms.

When: January - June 2016    Location:  Sauganash Elementary School

What:  We know not every child or family needs therapy to address their concerns, so we are now offering an additional service to help those families out.  We are offering short-term solutions with 20-, 40- and 60-minute sessions over the phone, video online, or in person to address a variety of concerns related to childhood and adolescents.  For more information, visit Consulting for Parents.                                 When: Effective December 2015

What:  Due to the perpetual CPS budget cuts (including social work and psychological services), we wanted to assist in some way. We will begin to donate our counseling services directly to students at neighborhood Chicago Public School (CPS).                                                                       When:  September 2015                                                                       Location:  Sauganash Elementary School

What:  We are pleased to announce our new name Concentric Counseling & Consulting to better reflect our counseling philosophy, mission, and services.   We were formerly Larson Counseling & Consultation, P.C.  When: April 2015

What:  We introduce offering compassion, empathy, guidance and options during your journey with infertility.  Review the flyer for more information.                                         When: June 2014

What: Through Facebook and Twitter, we offer another way to connect and share information on how to improve your and your families' well-being.                                                                                                              When: January 2014 

What:  Jennifer Larson, LCPC, NCC, is passionate about helping people to create and maintain a good self-care regimen!  For tips and pointers on how to get started, please read her article published in Neighborhood Parents Network's (NPN) Parent-to-Parent Newsletter.   When:  January 2014

 

What:  Larson Counseling & Consultation, P.C. becomes a group practice by expanding to include another therapist and additional services to include counseling and therapy to children and families, and supervision and consultation services.                                                                           When:  August 2013

What:  Tired of Feeling Tired? Workshop to All Working Moms
When: April 23, 2012 12:00pm-1:30pm
Location: Country Financial, 55 W. Monroe, Ste 3150, Chicago, IL 60603

Free workshop on how to set up boundaries and create a self-care plan to avoid feeling tired and burnout.  

What:  Tired of Feeling Tired? Speaking Engagement with NPN Working Moms
When: March 30, 2011 12:00pm-1:30pm
Location: Country Financial, 55 W. Monroe, Ste 3150, Chicago, IL 60603

Speaking Engagement with Working Moms Group of Neighborhood Parents Network (NPN). Obtain pointers on how you can increase your self-care and decrease burnout by establishing and maintaining healthy boundaries within the workplace and other areas of your life.  In this discussion workshop, you will learn about the purpose and function of boundaries and the value of prioritizing your needs and wants in order to achieve a more balanced and fulfilling life.
